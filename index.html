ABOUT TRICENT AEROSPACE

Engineered for
the Mission

TRICENT AEROSPACE is an Indian deep-tech aerospace company. We design, manufacture and support AI-enabled unmanned aircraft systems for defence, homeland security, critical infrastructure and industry.

Critical operations need more than standalone drones. They need platforms that sense reliably, systems that integrate cleanly, and outcomes that can be delivered in the field — under real conditions.

We build the aircraft, the mission systems, and the integration discipline between them — as one engineering problem.

MISSION

To design, build and support unmanned systems that critical operators can trust in the field — every flight, every time.

VISION

To be the benchmark for dependable, intelligent unmanned-systems engineering from India — trusted at home, respected globally.

PURPOSE

Turning airborne sensing into decisions people can act on — for the teams who protect borders, cities, infrastructure and industry.

OBJECTIVES

  • Deliver dependable unmanned platforms for critical operations.
  • Advance indigenous design and manufacturing of mission systems.
  • Ship one integrated architecture across every platform.
  • Support every aircraft through its full service life.

HEADQUARTERS

TRICENT AEROSPACE is headquartered in Lucknow, Uttar Pradesh, India, where design, manufacturing, systems integration and flight test operate from a single campus — including a captive UAS test zone for acceptance and demonstration flights.

HOW WE WORK

Indigenous engineering, deepened by international technology partnerships. Every aircraft is built, flown and accepted before delivery, and supported by us through its full service life.
